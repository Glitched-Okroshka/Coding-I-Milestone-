About the First Xbox
The first xbox was released in 2001, over a decade ago now, and was immediatedly put in competition with the Playstation 2 and GameCube. The xbox was created as a diverse strategy in order to capitalize in the gaming industy at the time and compete due to the concerns of the successful PlayStation 2 possibly pushing out the personal computer market.
