About the Xbox Series X
The Xbox Series X is the newest Xbox which was created in 2020 that offers great new generation performance that offers quick loading times and a vast library of playable games. The Xbox Series X displays functions that are more powerful than the past Xboxs such as parts of games you want to install, 4k streaming at 60 fps, capture privileges that can be sent straight to your phone.
