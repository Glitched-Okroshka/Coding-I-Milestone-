What is Microsoft Edge?

    Properties of Microsoft Edge

  • Microsoft Edge was created on April 4th, 1975 by Bill Gates and Paul Allen. It was released in 2015 and is considered one of the Big Five companies in the U.S.
  • Microsoft Edge is the default web browser for Windows.
  • Edge was created for the purpose of being the successor of Internet Explorer, but the 11th version stayed to keep Microsoft Edge compatibile
  • As of 2023, Edge is the third most popular browser in the world (below Safari and Chrome).
